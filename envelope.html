Typography on Envelope


This is a school project. We need to design five envelops with different typographic requirements. After the design of the envelopes were done, the teacher mailed all of them by Hong Kong Post. One of the task
requirements is to go crazy with the last envelope design and make sure that the postman cannot read the address and return it to you. I succeed for that and did not receive the last one which
is made of Bauhaus 93 typeface.

I utilized the geometric and monotone stroke weight of the font, by sticking the words together like a puzzle and formed a new shape. I also duplicate the linked shape of the Bauhaus font and make
an overlapping to form a more complicated outlook.
