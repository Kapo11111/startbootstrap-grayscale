Snapple Video Advertisement

It is colorful, it is youth.

In order to get closer to the target which is teenager, I hope they can found themselves in the advertising. As a result, I chose first love, public examination and friendship to be the contents of the
advertisements. Teenage must have experienced one of the three situations. Snapple drinks have a lot of different flavors and are presented by many different colors. I made use of this product
feature to create my advertising campaign. My thought is to choose three of the flavors and make linkage to each of the stories.


To let the advertisements become more impressive and not that ordinary, I put unexpected twists to each of the stories.
